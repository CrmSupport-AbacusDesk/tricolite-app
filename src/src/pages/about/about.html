Company Profile

ToyZone

  • Introduction

    Sanjay Mehndiratta, an 18 years old boy, with no money but with big dreams starts his toys business in 1986 by borrowing Rs.500.00 from a friend. By 1993, he starts importing toys from China and having established his name in trading, he realizes that there is not even one truly Indian Company which can boast to have ability to give competition to Chinese toys. He decides to promote Toy Zone Impex Private Limited in 2001 to start toys manufacturing business and take Chinese products head on.


    Finally, in 2004 the company starts its first manufacturing unit at Bhiwadi, Rajasthan under its own brand name ‘Toyzone’. The company started with just one category i.e., Ride On and very soon became pioneer in starting Magic Car a new category not produced so far in India. More than 34 lakh Magic Cars have been sold by the company since start.


    Today, to bring joys on the faces of little kids, the company has more than 1000 products in 45 categories from 4 manufacturing facilities owned by it and two facilities owned by the associates of the promoter. Probably, it is the largest toys manufacturer in MSME sector with 100% shareholding in the hands of an Indian making citizens of India proud.


    Toys are vital tools that help foster the mental, physical, emotional and social development of children. They are the basic instruments for the development of children’s fantasy, imagination and creativity. The company has been constantly innovating to fulfil children needs and necessities and towards this end, it has been exploring the possibility of new designs, technologies, new range and features. The efforts of the company have ensured and established its leadership in creating better toys for the future generation


    Most of the toys of the company conform to International Quality standards and have been exported to many countries including Japan after passing through their stringent quality tests. Government of India has notified that effective from 01.01.2021, all the toys to be sold in India whether produced in India or are imported must meet BIS Quality Norms and have to get the license to use BIS on the products. The products of the company have passed the quality norms of BIS and is licensed to use BIS mark on its products.


    The company has obtained licenses for all the major Cartoon characters sought after by the children and legitimately uses those characters on its products giving them opportunity to relate their toys with the characters of their preferred choice.


    To ensure timely supply of toys every nook and corner of the country, the company has its own distribution set up at Delhi, Mumbai, Pune, Kolkata, Bangalore and Hyderabad and reaches out to other areas through wholesale channels. It is the leading toys supplier to all the Modern Retail Trade Stores and E-commerce Stores.


    A reliable manufacturer and supplier of toys for kids of ages 6 months to 8 years. Wide selection of toys include Ride-ons, Magic Cars, Baby Walkers, Baby Strollers, Infant potty seats, Wave Rollers, Scooters, Tricycles, Rechargeables, Kitchen Sets, Doll Houses, Games & Sports and Furnitures etc. Safety without compromising quality is given utmost importance.
